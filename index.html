Donor Tier Segmentation

Upload a Givebutter CSV export to segment donors by giving level

How This Works

Step 1: Export Your Data from Givebutter

  1. Log into your Givebutter account
  2. Go to Transactions in the menu
  3. Click the Export button (usually in the top right)
  4. Choose CSV format and download the file

Step 2: Upload and Analyze

  1. Click the upload area above (or drag your file onto it)
  2. Select the CSV file you just downloaded
  3. Adjust the time period if needed (default is 12 months)
  4. Click "Analyze Donations"

Step 3: Review Your Donor Tiers

Your donors will be grouped by how much they've given:

  • Platinum - Your biggest supporters ($2,500+)
  • Gold - Major donors ($1,000+)
  • Silver - Generous givers ($500+)
  • Bronze - Regular supporters ($250+)
  • Friend - All other donors ($50+)

You can customize these tier names and amounts!

Merging Duplicate Donors

Sometimes the same person appears twice with different Contact IDs. To combine them:

  1. Check the boxes next to the donors you want to merge
  2. Click the "Merge Selected Donors" button that appears
  3. Their donations will be combined into one total

Your Data Stays Private

Your donor information never leaves your computer.

This tool runs entirely in your web browser. When you upload a file, it's processed right here on your computer - nothing is sent to the internet or stored on any server. When you close this page, the data is gone.
